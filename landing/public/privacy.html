Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Mirror — Natural Look ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Camera

The app accesses your device's front camera solely to display your reflection on screen. Camera data is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere. It is used only in real-time, locally on your device.

Purchase Information

If you purchase the $5 lifetime premium upgrade, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive a purchase token from Google to confirm your purchase and unlock premium features. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Advertising

The free version of the app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device's Google settings. Premium users see no ads.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal details
  • We do not record or store camera footage
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not track your location

Local Storage

The app stores the following locally on your device only: your installation date (to track the 30-day trial), and your premium purchase status. This data never leaves your device.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
